Pinch for Contractors

Pinch’s contractor profiles are for all types of contractors across a wide variety of specialties. Our comprehensive search features allow you to find projects suited to your specific interests and skillset, while also offering the opportunity to increase revenue with access to a wider selection of work. Included endorsement features also provide the opportunity to market your services to a larger audience.

Pinch also offers project management features that allow you to easily keep track of your projects from start to finish on your own schedule. We understand the importance of small businesses and self-employed professionals, which is why you won’t work for us. Our goal is to provide a platform for you to expand your business while you remain your own boss.
